NSi is Once Again Cycling in the ADA Tour de Cure

The NSi Cycling Team is once again riding for a cure in the American Diabetes Association's Tour de Cure on June 2nd in Reston, VA. The Tour de Cure is an annual ride held in 44 states that raises awareness and funds to help fight diabetes. Last year Team NSi raised $20K for the ADA, and this year we're setting the bar even higher with a goal of $30K!

Team NSi is dedicated to riding and raising money in this inspirational event because we personally know some of the 26 million Americans affected by this disease (and you might too). By making a donation, you will be supporting the American Diabetes Association mission to prevent and cure diabetes and improve the lives of all people affected by diabetes.

Will you help us change the future of diabetes? You can make a secure, 100% deductible, online donation to an individual rider or to the team as a whole by checking out our team page here: NSi Cycling Team Page

Thank you in advance for your generosity!
